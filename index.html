INTRODUCTION



Robert Westerveld is a young Dutchman, who currently lives and studies in Antwerp, Belgium. With almost six years of experience as a freelance music journalist , he has build up an impressive resume of articles and reviews, written for different media outlets.

On this website you will find a selection of his work, which mostly covers genres such as post-rock, metal, and experimental music. However, with a diverse music taste, and a broad knowledge of culture and literature, he is not afraid to venture outside the areas of his expertise.
